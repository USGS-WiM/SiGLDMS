Sites:
Spreadsheet mode works much like Excel; each row is an individual site. You can highlight and copy multiple cells or rows, or you can click and drag from the lower right cell corner to populate an entire column. You can also copy and paste directly from the Excel SiGL Site Worksheet.
Right-click any cell to add or remove a row (site). Drag column headers to adjust column widths.
Gray cells cannot be modified. Some cells have data validation applied and will turn red if the value entered does not meet the requirements. Red cells must be resolved before saving. The more sites, the longer the save will take. Please be patient.

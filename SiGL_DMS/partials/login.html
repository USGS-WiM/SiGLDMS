This Data Management System (DMS) supports the Science in the Great Lakes (SiGL) Mapper, which is a metadata-discovery tool that captures information about a wide range of scientific activities occuring in the Great Lakes and provides details on how to access associated data and products.

You can use the SiGL DMS to submit, edit, and delete your project and site information.
